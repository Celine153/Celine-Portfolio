OMG! Oh My Grammar Description

OMG! Oh My Grammar" is the culmination of a project that my group and I completed for our end-of-semester examination in the Artificial Intelligence course. We developed an AI implementation aimed at transforming informal language text into formal language text, aligning with the field of PKM-KC (Intellectual Property Creation). To provide access to our AI-driven idea, we created a website called "OMG! Oh My Grammar.
